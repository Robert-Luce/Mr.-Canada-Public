Montreal, the fashion capital of Canada, is a vibrant cultural hub that attracts creatives worldwide. With its European charm reflected in cobblestone streets, iconic landmarks like Basilique Notre-Dame, and cozy cafes in Plateau Mont-Royal, there's much to explore. Indulge in vintage shopping across the city or embrace seasonal activities like hiking in summer and enjoying sugar shacks in late winter and early spring, a delightful tradition rooted in Indigenous communities.
